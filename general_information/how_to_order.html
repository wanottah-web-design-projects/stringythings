How to Order
 

All prices quoted on our website and order totals calculated are in Pounds Sterling (£).

Orders can be placed via the Internet, mail, telephone or e-mail.

Payment can be made by Visa, Mastercard, Delta, Switch or Solo cards, or PayPal. Alternatively, for UK orders only, you can also pay by cheque or postal order.

If paying by cheque or postal order, please ensure they are made payable to 'STRINGYTHINGS.com'.

If you wish to place your order via e-mail, please send your order to

If you are ordering by mail, please send your order to:

STRINGYTHINGS.com
18 Plovers Way
Hockwold
Norfolk
IP26 4LF
United Kingdom

To order by telephone, please call us on +44 (0)1842 827897 Monday - Friday between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. GMT.

If you are paying by credit or debit card, for security purposes, we will only despatch orders to the registered cardholders address.

If you choose to pay by cheque, your order will not be dispatched until the cheque has been cleared by your bank, normally 4 working days.

If you choose to pay by postal order, your order will be dispatched upon receipt of payment.

Shipping
UK delivery: £3.50 ($7) per kit, 3-5 working days
International shipping: £20 ($40) per kit, 7-10 working days

Prices quoted are per order, excluding bulk orders.

If you are ordering on-line, this is automatically added to your order in the shopping cart.
