Cookies

Cookies are pieces of data stored with your temporary internet files by your web browser. The information contained within these cookies does not include, nor do they obtain any personally identifiable information such as your e-mail address, or any other information stored on your hard drive, and are used solely for the purpose of applets and special effects to function properly on the website and to identify your computer to a website upon your return.

Cookie technology is used when you visit our site to help us track various activities on the site. This includes which pages you visit, how many times you visit, how long you spend visiting, and how to recognise you as a 'user'.

Should you wish, you may prevent cookies from being stored by your web browser by adjusting your browser's security settings.

Please note: Disabling cookies will prevent certain features of the website from operating correctly.
